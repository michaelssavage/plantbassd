The weekend is starting a day earlier with a stacked St. Patrick's Day opening up the club proceedings this week in the capital.

## WEDNESDAY 16th March

<GigGuide
  title="
OVERTIME: Dr Banana, Harvey Furness b2b Misbeats @ Bongo Club"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-overtime.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1502624"
>
  Overtime bring back Dr Banana to Edinburgh weeks after his last show at Sneaky Pete's. One of the most prominent acts to bloom from the UKG revival, he takes on the Bongo on Paddy's eve alongside Harvey Furness and Misbeats. Very generously, 50% of profits on the night will go to supporting Ukraine.

</GigGuide>

## THURSDAY 17th March (St. Patrick's Day)

<GigGuide
  title="Greenhouse Records: Lux @ Sneaky Pete's"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-greenhouse.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1498831"
>
  Flourishing from the growing Leipzig scene, Lux lands to Sneaky's for the first Greenhouse show of
  the year. Having played down many famous dancefloors like Panorama Bar & de school, you can expect
  a raucous run down of all things Detroit and UK.
</GigGuide>

## THURSDAY 17th March (St. Patrick's Day)

<GigGuide
  title="Nocturnal Rhythms with Interplanetary Criminal b2b Bluetoof & more @ Bongo Club"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-nocturnal.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1501882"
>
  London based club night Nocturnal Rhythms takes its show north after finding its home at Corsica
  Studios with a bumper St. Patrick's Day party. Interplanetary Criminal and Bluetoof lock horns for
  a mouth watering b2b, with support across the two rooms from city locals J Wax, Feena, Moray
  Leisure Centre & more. One of our picks for the week!
</GigGuide>

## THURSDAY 17th March (St. Patrick's Day)

<GigGuide
  title="Club Nacht presents Swoose @ Mash House"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-swoose.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1508813 "
>
  A Scottish debut for Belfast native and AVA mainstay as Swoose is Club Nacht's main feature this
  Paddy's Day. A master of euphoric, rave-infused house, Swoose has released on esteemed labels like
  Shall Not Fade in recent years as well as featuring at the likes of Fabric, Printworks, and more
  huge UK venues. Support across two rooms from Edinburgh's brightest spinners.
</GigGuide>

## FRIDAY 18th March

<GigGuide
  title="Overground presents Rave for Ukraine @ Mash House"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-ukraine.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1507548 "
>
  Overground return after a stellar night with Coco Bryce at Bongo last week for a humanitarian rave
  for Ukraine. Residents from Overground, Milkit, Epika, & Jon Krieger take the reins across the two
  rooms of Mash. Expect all things rave, breaks, acid, electro, & beyond.
</GigGuide>

## FRIDAY 18th March

<GigGuide
  title="Aquelarre: Lonely Carp & Pako Vega @ Sneaky Pete's"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-aquelarre.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1503161"
>
  Aquelarre's first party of the year is a local affair with Lonely Carp (Spit It Out/ EHFM), and
  Pako Vega. Lonely Carp will be sound voyaging the music that has accompanied her life as a trans
  artist through double bass, field recordings, and queer industrial music alongisde Pako Vega's
  trippy leftfield and electro beats.
</GigGuide>

## FRIDAY 18th March

<GigGuide
  title="Electrikal Present: IMANU/ ANNIX/ ANIKONIK/ VOLENS CHORUS @ Bongo Club"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-electrikal.jpg"

## SATURDAY 19th March

<GigGuide
  title="ECFS Caves Special: Steve Cox + Night Tube Residents @ the Caves"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-caves.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1508183"
>
  Notable Glasgow DJ and Sub Club resident Stevie Cox is the special guest for ECFS's biggest show
  to date with a helping hand from the Night Tube residents. Electro, breaks and techno with
  proceeds going towards StrutSafe and Mellon Educate.
</GigGuide>

## SATURDAY 19th March

<GigGuide
  title="Heal Yourself & Move @ Sneaky Pete's"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-hyam.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1504117"
>
  Heal Yourself & Move presents being (live), Other lANDS & Housse of Traps. 'being' is one of the
  city's most elusive producers of sounds and takes on a very special live set which is to be
  followed by dj sets from Otherlands & House of Traps. A sonic transmission is expected to range

</GigGuide>

## SUNDAY 20th March

<GigGuide
  title="Postal presents Chloé Robinson @ Sneaky Pete's"
  pic="/news/club-guide/08-chloe.jpg"
  link="ra.co/events/1510403"
>
  Rinse FM and NTS regular Chloé Robinson arrives to Postal Sunday's packed with an arsenal of
  techno, electro, breaks, and UKG. With performances at the likes of Glastonbury, Printworks, and
